About Us

ACME Accountants Limited provides expert advice and high-class services with a personal touch. Davey Jones BA FCA, founding director:

A qualified chartered accountant since 2002, Dave is highly skilled in business planning, tax advising and financial accounting. Having worked in a variety of finance roles, his strong professional history gives you direct access to a wealth of tax knowledge.

Prior to founding Sunrise Accountants, Dave spent two years providing technical tax advice and compliance support for accountancy practices. Before this role, he worked in industry for 15 years for a worldwide manufacturing group of companies. Dave had a broad range of responsibilities, which included preparing both management and statutory accounts and analysing costs against budgets.
