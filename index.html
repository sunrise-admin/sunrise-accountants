About Us

Sunrise Accountants Limited provides expert advice and high-class services with a personal touch. We will get to know you or your business in detail, to offer tailored advice regarding business models, profitability, weaknesses and more. We will ensure that all your accountancy requirements are taken care of in a tax efficient manner.

Rob Sheehan BA FCA, founding director:

A qualified chartered accountant since 2002, Rob is highly skilled in business planning, tax advising and financial accounting. Having worked in a variety of finance roles, his strong professional history gives you direct access to a wealth of tax knowledge.

Prior to founding Sunrise Accountants, Rob spent two years providing technical tax advice and compliance support for accountancy practices. Before this role, he worked in industry for 15 years for a worldwide manufacturing group of companies. Rob had a broad range of responsibilities, which included preparing both management and statutory accounts and analysing costs against budgets.
